Just takes one look to fall in love , the Kashmiri pure cotton silk Kani unstitched suit handwoven with fine Kashmiri details by the artisans of Kashmir valley . The odhani has beautiful handwoven zari Patola motifs all over. It comes with the bottoms.

Zari comes from the Persian word for gold : Zar . Zari is produced by twisting a flattened metallic strip made from pure gold , silver or a metallic polyester film around a yarn. This thread is then used to render intricate motifs and patterns on luxurious fabrics. Kani reflects the work of excellent craftsmanship by the skilled artisans
